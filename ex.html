See some of my favourite artists depending on my mood.

Jinsang

He is californian beatmaker who specializes in soft and dazed hip-hop tracks with elements sourced from soul and jazz recordings.My favourite albums of his are "Blossoms", which he made with another great producer called Swum, and "Confessions".

AWAY

He is an electronic music producer with a unique sound. He combines electronic and rock sounds creating dark and gritty songs.Some of my facourites are "Too close" and "Hole in the earth".

Nujabes

A Japanese producer, DJ that combined hip-hop beats with jazz creating atmospheric instrumental mixes. Considered one of the first to music that sounds similar to the modern Lofi music. Some of my favourite tracks include "World's end Rhapsody" and "Aruarian dance".

Gesaffelstein

A French DJ and producer who is known for his dark and threatening techno which goes back to the roots of the genre. My favourites include "Opr" and "Viol".

Yung bae

He is a future funk producer who has a upbeat and fun sound. He frequently samples 1980s Japanese city pop and his music is described as groovy. Some of my favourites are "Ain't nobody like you" and "Must be love".

Porter Robinson

A very popular electro-house producer whose music has a lot of his own personality and soul. He also has a side project called "Virtual self" with a completly diffrent yet familiar sound. My favourite songs are "Shelter" and "Goodbye to a world".
